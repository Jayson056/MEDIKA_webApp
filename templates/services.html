Medika Features

Medika offers a wide range of features carefully designed to simplify and streamline the management of patient health records. With its user-friendly interface and reliable functionality, it enables seamless handling of important medical data, ensuring that records are efficiently and accurately maintained. By focusing on key tasks, Medika provides an organized and effective way to manage health-related information with ease. Below are the core functions that make Medika an essential tool:

Add New Patient

Create records with essential details like name, student number, health assessment, and visit date.

Search Patient Record

Quickly find patient info using their student number.

Update Patient Record

Edit existing records to keep data accurate and current.

View All Patient Records

Access and review all stored patient records in one place.
